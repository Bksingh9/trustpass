# Monetization

TRUSTPASS makes vendors procurement-ready and helps buyers find verified vendors faster. Revenue comes from recurring subscriptions plus trust verification services. Productized recurring models work best when value is standardized, visible, and tied to real business outcomes.

## Revenue Lines

- Vendor subscriptions for profile, document vault, verification workflow, reminders, and badges.
- Assisted verification packs for vendors that want guided document readiness.
- Buyer access plans for search, shortlists, request tracking, and team workflows.
- Referral or onboarding fees where TRUSTPASS helps route qualified vendors into buyer procurement workflows.
- Later API/widget monetization for embedding trust badges or vendor trust summaries in partner systems.

## Initial Customer Profile

Best starting customers:

- Service vendors selling into mid-market buyers.
- Agencies and consultants that repeatedly answer procurement due diligence.
- Logistics and operations vendors with recurring document requirements.
- Buyers with fragmented vendor onboarding and manual document review.

## Unit Economics Assumptions

- Vendor subscription should cover document storage, reminders, profile hosting, and basic support.
- Assisted verification packs should cover human review time with margin.
- Buyer plans should be priced around time saved by procurement teams and reduced onboarding risk.
- Admin review efficiency is critical because verification service margin depends on review throughput.

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

### 30 Days

- Recruit 10 to 20 vendors in one or two categories.
- Manually assist onboarding to learn missing checklist rules.
- Validate buyer-safe trust summary language.

### 60 Days

- Add buyer teams to search and shortlist verified vendors.
- Measure vendor completion rate, review time, and buyer request volume.
- Convert manual verification into priced packs.

### 90 Days

- Launch paid vendor subscriptions.
- Introduce buyer team access.
- Build category-specific proof points and case studies.
- Explore API/widget demand from buyers and partner networks.
